## Key Points

- The State of Delaware Division of Public Health seeks professional services to establish mobile health units (Component A) and/or brick-and-mortar health hubs (Component B) in underserved rural areas of Sussex and Kent Counties to improve healthcare access and outcomes.
- Proposals due April 17, 2026 at 1:00 PM EDT; applicants may bid for Component A only, Component B only, or both components.
- Initiative emphasizes five core goals: expanding healthcare access, improving health outcomes through early detection, advancing health equity via data-driven deployment, strengthening healthcare system integration, and establishing sustainable infrastructure.
- Vendors must demonstrate relevant rural healthcare experience, partnerships with FQHCs and community organizations, and comprehensive sustainability strategies addressing long-term operational viability.
- Evaluation criteria (100 points total) prioritize sustainability planning (10 points), administrative oversight (7 points), relevant experience (7 points), and alignment with RFP goals (7 points), with detailed scoring across 22 dimensions.
- Contract term is one year with four optional one-year extensions; selected vendor(s) must carry specific insurance minimums including $1,000,000 commercial general liability and automotive liability coverage.

## Entities and Topics

- **Division of Public Health (DPH)**: Delaware state agency issuing the RFP to address rural healthcare disparities.
- **Sussex and Kent Counties**: Target rural communities in Delaware for service deployment.
- **Mobile Health Units (Component A)**: Preventive, primary, behavioral health, specialty, and health education services delivered directly to communities.
- **Brick-and-Mortar Health Hubs (Component B)**: Fixed-site facilities providing comprehensive healthcare services in accessible rural locations.
- **Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs)**: Required partnership entities for clinical integration and continuity of care.
- **Electronic Medical Records (EMRs)**: Technology integration requirement for clinical data management.
- **Delaware Health Information Network (DHIN)**: State health information exchange system for integration.
- **CDC Social Vulnerability Index (SVI)**: Data tool for identifying communities with greatest access barriers.
- **Make Rural America Healthy Again**: Strategic initiative alignment goal referenced in proposal objectives.
- **Euna Procurement (formerly Bonfire)**: Online portal for proposal submission and communication.
